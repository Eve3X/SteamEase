Our Approach

Clothes maketh a man and define his place in the social ecosystem. The need for suitably cleaned and pressed clothes is the basic necessity of the modern world. The personal and professional demands have forced consumers to seek assistance from experts in the laundry industry to ensure seamless solutions. Crease Art, the pioneer in the business of laundry services has created an impact in the society through efficient operations and timely services.

Our founders have understood and analysed the hindrances faced by our consumers in this sector and have formulated the ideal solutions to combat them. We are launching our specialised mobile application for laundry services through which we assist our consumers in identifying the right laundry vendor for them. The application is user-friendly and has deployed an advanced technology to enrich consumer experience. We have created every aspect of the application with extreme consideration. This platform will successfully bridge the gap between the dry-cleaning and laundry service providers and consumers.

Customised Solutions

Transparency
